3b.
Why are there coating defects?
Because the coating is not applied evenly to the surface.

Why the coating is not applied evenly?
Because the paint machine spray output is not stable

Why are the outputs not stable ?
After inspection its found that air pressure sensors have not been calibrated according to the maintenance schedule. Thus resulting the machine to operate with fluctuative air pressure, while still showign the "normal air pressure level" on the display.
